WBEBL
The creation of (WBEBL) on April 4th, 2023 is the West Bengal Electricity Board Limited in WB (India).The main business of WBEBL is distribution and hydro generation of electricity.It is also the nodal agency of the goverment of west bengal for undertaking rural electrification task in the state with object of providing access of electricity to all rural households in the state. Over the years, WBEBC has been continually evolving to position itself as a leading Power Utility services organization of the Country. In the changing landscape, post recent global slowdown, it is anticipated that demand for electricity will change rapidly and expectations will also increase exponentially. WBEBL continually strives to enhance the quality of services delivered to its customers. WBEBL provides quality power to a gargantuan customer base of more than 2.03 crore across West Bengal through its service network spanning 5 Zones, 20 Regional Offices, 76 Distribution Divisions and 534 Customer Care Centers.
